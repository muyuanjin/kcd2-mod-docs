# Modding rules
**Kingdom Come: Deliverance II Steam Workshop Guidelines**

1. **Appropriate content marking:** if your mod includes mature content (like nudity, gore etc.) clearly mark it as such.

2. **Originality:** upload only your original work. Do not re-upload existing mods or use content from other creators without proper permission.

3. **Respectful content:** mods containing discriminatory content or encouraging harmful behavior have no place here.

4. **No malicious code:** mods must not contain harmful elements like exploits, backdoors, or any code that could negatively affect other players' experiences.

5. **Avoid external dependencies:** design mods to function independently without requiring additional downloads.

6. **Accurate tagging:** use appropriate tags to describe your mod, ensuring users can find and understand its purpose easily.

7. **Proper thumbnails:** provide clear and relevant thumbnails for your mods to give users a visual preview.

8. **No advertisements:** refrain from including intrusive advertisements, paid promotions, or external monetization methods within your mods.

9. **Follow Steam’s rules:** all uploaded content must comply with [Steam's Subscriber Agreement](http://store.steampowered.com/subscriber_agreement/), [Steam’s Online Conduct Rules](https://store.steampowered.com/online_conduct/) and [Rules and Guidelines For Steam: Discussions, Reviews, and User Generated Content](https://help.steampowered.com/en/faqs/view/6862-8119-C23E-EA7B). Mods that violate these rules (like copyright infringement, illegal content etc.) will be removed.




By following these guidelines, we aim to foster a creative and respectful environment for all Kingdom Come: Deliverance II modders and players.
