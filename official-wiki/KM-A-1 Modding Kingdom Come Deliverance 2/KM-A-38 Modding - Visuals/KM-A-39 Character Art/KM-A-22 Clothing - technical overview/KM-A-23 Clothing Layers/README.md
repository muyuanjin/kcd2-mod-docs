# **Torso**

* ## **Body garment slot**

### **Non-gambeson items**

Various tunics, jupons and pourpoints must meet the following requirements:

* Small pouch - hideable geo.
* Large pouch - hideable geo.
* Secondary sword belt. This belt is picked from available secondary belts in macro atlas. It is a hideable geo.

### **Gambeson items**

Various types of gambesons, aketons, padded jackets and so on must meet the following requirements:

* Small pouch - hideable geo.
* Large pouch - hideable geo.
* Feature for chainmails (refer to chainmails for details).
* Secondary sword belt. This belt is picked from available secondary belts in macro atlas. It is a hideable geo.
* ## **Body chainmail slot**

Only chainmails. Chaimails do not have their own geo they are just a paintable feature in every gambeson.

* Two skirt lenghts - short and long with feature for endings.
* Two sleeve lengths - short and long with feature for endings.
* ## **Body plate slot**

Cuirasses and brigandines must meet the following requirements:

* Small pouch - hideable geo.
* Large pouch - hideable geo.
* Must include gambeson geometry of the most common length.
* Must include chainmail features.
* Other length is achieved via skirt attachment on the waist seam.
* ## **Coat layer**

Waffenrocks and coats must meet the following requirements:

* Small pouch - hideable geo.
* Large pouch - hideable geo.
* Three different lenghts of sleeves: none, short, long.
* Must include gambeson geometry of the most common length.
* Other length is achieved via skirt attachment on the waist seam.
* Puffable morph to simulate the presence of cuirass or brigandine.
* Check compatibility with arm armor.




# **Arms**

* ## **Arm armour**

Vambraces, couters and full plate arm armour.

* Must include short sleeve coat geometry.
* Must include rolled up long sleeve geometry (attached to short sleeve geo).
* Must include gambeson feature.
* Must include a chainmail feature.
* If there are pauldrons they must have their own feature to by hideable.
* Also, there must by morph to push then over large padded/mail coif.
* Standardized length and ending to fit into the gauntlet opening.
* ## **Gloves**

Leather and gauntlets.

* Wide opening to accommodate arm armor.




# **Legs**

* ## **Hose slot**

### **Non-gambeson types**

Different types of hose.

* Unified ankle seam.
* Unified hip seam.
* Volume for skirts
* Volume for knee high boots.

### **Gambeson types**

Padded legs and hose must follow these requirements:

* Unified ankle seam.
* Unified hip seam.
* Volume for skirts
* Seams for variable geometry for boots of various heights or volume for knee higgh boots. Depends on thickness of the padding.
* ## **Leg Plate slot**

Birgandine, mail or plate leg protection, must follow these requirements:

* If not completely enclosed they must have feature for ankle and knee-high "tucked-in" boots.
* If they do not have a sabaton (attached metal shoe) they must fit on the ankle seam.
* Separate model for chainmail protection.
* ## **Boots slot**

Boots, shoes, leather soles and so on.

* Three different shoe sizes. Low-cut, ankle-high, knee-high.
* Cut to three different features respectively to boot sizes.
* If you wear armor without a sabaton, everything from ankle seam up will be hidden and in plate legs an appropriate boot feature will become visible.
* All boots must have a hideable spur geo.
* ## **Spurs**
* All shoes must have a hideable spur geometry.
* Feet also have a hidden spur geo.




# **Head**

* ## **Coif slot**

Cloth coif, padded small and large and mail coif.

### **Non-gambeson types**

* Just cloth coif
* Exists as a separate geo

### **Gambeson types**

* Large mail/padded coif and small padded coif
* Large ones have shrink morph to accommodate for hood
* Exists as a separate geo
* ## **Collar slot**
* Unified neck seam
* Must fit under long padded/mail coifs
* ## **Hood slot**
* Morph to accomodate for large coifs
* Has hooded and unhooded variant. These are two different models.




* ## **Helmet slot**

### **Hats and caps**

* All sorts of types.
* Have geo for coif - hideable.

### **Helmets**

* All sorts of types.
* Kettle-hats and skullcaps, and bascinets without an aventail have mail and padded large and small padded coif geo in.
* Visored helmets have a standardized bone for the visor animation.
